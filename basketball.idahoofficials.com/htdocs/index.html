NFHS Exam

Exam for 2020-2021 Season will open on 10/23/2020 and close on 11/24/2020. Link to NFHS Exams.
The exam is open book. Great way to find some buddies and go over questions together.
  • 70% required to be a subvarsity official
  • 80% required to be a varsity official
  • 85% required to be eligible for post season
